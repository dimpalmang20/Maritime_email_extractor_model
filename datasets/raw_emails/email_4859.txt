ALL DETAILS ABOUT


All speed and consumption warranties are said to be "about" which is
interpreted as +5% on consumption and - 5% on speed. The vessel is entitled
to use more gasoil when the vessel is manoeuvring in shallow / narrow
waters, entering and leaving port and in heavy weather and starting /
stopping Main Engine.

The Vessel's speed and consumption is to be warranted and determined on the
basis of "Good Weather Days" which are
defined as continuous periods from noon until successive noon during which
conditions do not at any time:
- Exceed Beaufort Scale 4 (up to and including 16 knots); and, or
- Exceed Douglas Sea State 3 (combined wind driven sea and swell up to and
including 1.25m); and, or
- Include any negative / adverse currents; and, or
- Include periods within 24 hours from the commencement of a sea passage;
and, or
- Include periods within 24 hours of the completion of a sea passage; and,
or
- Include periods where the Vessel's speed is reduced due to narrow /
restricted waters, poor visibility, for safety reasons in the Master's
discretion,
due to Charterers'   instructions or when the Vessel complies with local
authority regulations / regulations / mandated speeds; and, or
- Include periods when vessel operates in ECA areas where only LSMGO can be
used in M/E.

Any periods which do not qualify as "Good Weather Days" are to be excluded
from calculations regarding the Vessel's performance and are not subject to
a speed and performance claim from Charterers. No extrapolation whatsoever
of "Good Weather Days" is allowed. Favourable currents are not to be taken
into account and are to be excluded from calculations regarding the Vessel's
performance.

Vessel's performance to be calculated on the basis of round voyage basis
including the ballast and laden leg. Any proven underperformance and/or
overconsumption, to be off-set by any over-performance and/or
under-consumption respectively at the same or other voyage. Under no
circumstances whatsoever are Charterers entitled to deduct any alleged
performance claim from hire. In the event of a performance claim, Charterers
are to submit their claim to Owners for agreement. Any speed/consumption
claim to be submitted latest within 30 days after completion of the
respective voyage otherwise to become null and void.
