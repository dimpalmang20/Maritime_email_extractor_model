\'EUROPEAN EMISSIONS SURCHARGE\' (EUES)
NOTICE OF ADJUSTMENT
Dear Customer,
In accordance with the
European Union Emissions Trading System & FUEL EU Maritime Regulation
, AAL is applying a combined ‘European Emissions Surcharge’ (EUES) to cargo either loaded on, or already onboard, an AAL-operated vessel sailing either to or from a European port.
The EUES is applicable to part cargoes and based on typical vessel routing, average fuel consumption, and cargo volumes - as well as the prevailing EU Emissions Allowance trading price. In line with latest published European Emissions Tariffs, it has been adjusted as of
1 January 2026
